Got Questions?

Frequently asked

How quickly will a driver arrive?▼
We guarantee a driver at your location within 30 minutes anywhere in Raipur, Bhilai, Durg, or Bilaspur. Often 15–20 minutes.
Is the service available at night?▼
Absolutely. DriveBuddy operates 24 hours, 7 days a week. Our night safety driver service (8 PM – 6 AM) is popular with women, families, and late-night commuters.
Difference between Local and Outstation?▼
Local (₹1000–1200) covers full-day city driving. Outstation (₹1200–1500) is for travel beyond city limits — call 9111473929 for a custom quote.
What if I need to extend my booking?▼
You can extend directly with the driver or by calling us. Extension charges are per-hour. The driver stays until the job is done.
How are drivers verified?▼
Every driver completes a 4-stage verification: Government ID, police background check, breathalyzer before every shift, and 5+ years experience.
What payment methods do you accept?▼
Cash, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), and bank transfer. Simple and transparent — zero hidden charges.
Can I book for hospital emergencies?▼
Yes — medical and emergency bookings are our absolute priority. Call 9111473929 immediately for instant dispatch.
How does the online booking form work?▼
Fill in your details and submit — your booking is saved directly to Firebase and our team is notified instantly. We'll call within minutes.
