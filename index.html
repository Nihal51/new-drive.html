Got Questions?

Frequently asked

How quickly will a driver arrive? ▼
We guarantee a driver at your location within 30 minutes of booking, anywhere in Raipur, Bhilai, Durg, or Bilaspur. In most cases, drivers arrive even faster — often 15–20 minutes.
Is the service available at night? ▼
Absolutely. DriveBuddy operates 24 hours, 7 days a week including all public holidays. Our night safety driver service (8 PM – 6 AM) is specifically popular with women, families, and late-night office commuters.
What happens if I need more time? ▼
You can extend your booking directly with the driver or by calling us. Extension charges are calculated on a per-hour basis. The driver is with you until the job is done — no rushing.
Do you provide outstation drivers? ▼
Yes! We provide outstation drivers for long-distance trips. Rates depend on the destination and duration. Call us on 9111473929 or WhatsApp for a custom quote for your outstation trip.
How are drivers verified? ▼
Every driver completes a 4-stage verification: government ID check, police background check, breathalyzer test, and a 5+ year professional driving experience requirement. We also conduct periodic re-verification.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept cash, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), and bank transfers. Payment is made directly to us — simple and transparent with no hidden charges whatsoever.
Can I book for hospital emergencies? ▼
Yes — medical and emergency bookings are our priority. Call 9111473929 immediately. We dispatch the nearest available driver to you as fast as possible, any hour of the day or night.
Which cities do you currently serve? ▼
We currently provide full coverage in Raipur, Bhilai, Durg, and Bilaspur. We are Chhattisgarh's first driver-on-demand platform and are actively expanding to more cities very soon.
